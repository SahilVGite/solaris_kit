HelioFlow™ Solar Collector

Meet HelioFlow™, the world’s first flat-packed solar collector engineered to provide carbon free solar hot water for multiple applications.

Modern, Attractive, and Reliable

HelioFlow™ features a sleek, prismatic design that sits low on flat roofs or ground mounts, offering a modern alternative to bulky, traditional solar collectors.

Engineered for safe thermal performance and compatibility with hard or mineralised water, it delivers a reliable, efficient solar hot water solution suitable for diverse environments worldwide.

Easy to Deploy. Simple to Install.

HelioFlow™ is the world’s first flat-packed, self-assembled solar collector. Assembled in approximately 20-minutes without any tools or training, its designed to let almost anyone build there very own EU certified solar thermal collector. Harness the energy of the sun today.

HelioFlow™ Specifications

  • DIMENSIONS

    520 x 520 x 170mm (Packaged)

    510 x 510 x 460mm (Assembled)

  • PERFORMANCE SPEC.

    5 bar working pressure, Maximum fluid

    temperature = 90 degrees Celsius

  • MATERIALS SPEC.

    High transmission solar glass (glazing),

    UV stabilised polymer (absorber)

  • CERTIFICATION

    Solar Keymark Certified

    (EN12975 / ISO9806)
