Efficient. Scalable.
Made for the Gulf.

The SolarisKit HelioPro solution is a complete, high-performance solar water heating system designed specifically for flat-roof buildings and Gulf climate conditions. Built around our patented HelioFlow prismatic solar collector, the HelioPro system delivers reliable hot water all year round while dramatically reducing electricity consumption and carbon emissions.

Whether you’re a developer seeking compliance and differentiation, an ESCO pursuing measurable energy savings, a consultant designing sustainable systems, or a distributor expanding into clean technology, HelioPro provides a modern, easy-to-integrate solution that works.

  • Why HelioPro?

    • Flat-roof compatible and architecturally clean – blends seamlessly with villa and commercial rooftops
    • Made in the UAE – ensuring quality, compliance, and sustainability
    • Modular and scalable – from single villas to large developments
    • Avoids excessive temperatures – ensuring longevity, safety, and reliability
    • Plug-and-play installation – faster setup, easier maintenance
