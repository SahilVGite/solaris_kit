What we do

Heating water accounts for up to 20% of household energy consumption

Meeting hot water demand is energy and carbon intensive when using electricity. A typical household consuming 300 litres of hot water daily will annually consume:

  • ~6,000kWh of Electricity

  • ~2,400kgs of carbon

Installing a solar water heater can lower energy costs and carbon, but current designs aren't designed for the Gulf

Using solar energy to heat water is efficient and sustainable, but current solar water heaters aren't optimised for flat-roofs and sunny climates.

  • Bulky and Obtrusive design

  • Reach excessive temperatures

  • Damaged by hard water
